About Me

LESLEY

She is a movement artist, choreographer, and director. She graduated in the Spring of 2018 with Interdisciplinary Bachelor's of Arts from Columbia College investigating the connection between dance and theatre. She is researching finding universal human connection through personal truths and shared experiences using the mediums of the written word (poetry, prose, play) and visual form (dance, theatre, film).
